Fair Pricing

In order to provide a fair pricing model, we don't have any sort of tier system. Just pay for what you use.

Staff People

Each staff person costs $1/month.

Your first 10 people are free!

Devices

Staff can have up to 5 devices.

Staff with more(up to 10), cost $2/month.

Admins

You get two admins strait out of the box.

Additional admins are $1/month.

Getting Started

Ideally this is free for a lot of small organizations.

The starting tier is 10 staff people.
